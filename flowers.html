begonia flower

The orange begonia flower is one of the flowers that must be planted in direct or partial sunlight, depending on its type, and needs irrigation with a large amount of water, and begins flowering at the beginning of the end of spring and continues to the beginning of winter, and needs fertile soil The area is well ventilated and does not require constant maintenance

bird of paradise flower

The Bird of Paradise flower is one of the flowers that must be planted in direct sunlight, and it needs a growth period that takes 5-8 years to grow and bloom, and it needs fertile, moist and well-drained soil in the growing season, note The homeland of this flower is South Africa

california poppy

The California Poppy is one of the flowers that must be planted in direct sunlight, but it does not need to be irrigated with large amounts of water, and usually blooms at the beginning of spring and summer, then the roses fall, due to the summer heat, and the rest remains The plant, which usually grows in coastal areas, needs sandy, well-drained soil

nemesia flower

The white nemesia flower is a perennial, extended, aromatic flower that usually grows at the end of May, and continues until the end of September, and is characterized by producing a large amount of flowers, so that the flowers cover the leaves of the tree, and must be placed Under direct sunlight.

tulip flower

The white tulip flower is a perennial flower, and it is preferable to plant it under direct sunlight, leaving a distance between each seedling of at least 5 cm, and it is preferable to plant it in the fall until it blooms in the spring. Tulip flower has 3 sepals, 3 petals shaped into a slim cup.

moon flower

Moonflower is characterized by its trumpet-like shape, pleasant aromatic smell, and grows in the form of a pergola, and usually blooms in summer and continues until autumn, preferably in partially sunny places. The moon flower is characterized by the fact that its flowers usually bloom after sunset, and its intense aromatic scent spreads throughout the evening

daffodil flower

The white daffodil flower is distinguished by its shape; They are white flowers with trumpet-shaped crowns, and have 6 petals, with a little yellow, and are usually planted at the end of autumn, and begin to bloom at the beginning of spring, and preferably in a sunny area, knowing that they need well-drained soil fertile, moist

dahlia flower

The white dahlia flower is distinguished by its two sizes; Small, with a diameter of about 5 cm, and large, with a diameter of about 30 cm, and it is a perennial flower, and it blooms in the middle of summer, and continues until autumn, and it needs exposure to partial sunlight, not direct, Despite the beauty of this flower, However, it does not have an obvious aromatic odor

iris flower

The white iris flower is distinguished by its long stem, and the need for exposure to direct sunlight to thrive, as it usually blooms from the beginning of spring until the beginning of summer. There are several types that are suitable for all types of soil, some of which are planted in well-drained soil, moist soil, water-rich soil, or rocky lands.

Euphorbia Flower

Verbena is a small purple flower with 5 small petals, native to South America, a flower that does not tolerate cold weather, and blooms in the hot summer. It is also a perennial flower, and there are more than 250 species available, and it needs well-drained acidic soil.

Lilac Flower

The lilac flower grows on a shrub that reaches a height of about 7 m, and its colors vary from light violet to purple, and they are arranged in the form of attractive and beautiful clusters, and are characterized by their wonderful and strong aromatic smell. Lilac blooms in mid-spring, and continues to the end, and the lilac flower is a fast-growing flower, very hardy, and does not require care.
